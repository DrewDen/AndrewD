About me

My passion for web development began after building my first website. Wanting to learn more about layout and design used to optimize website interaction, I started learning coding, which developed into a keen interest in web creation. I have fifteen years of management experience, which included supervising from five to twenty employees, all with varied learning styles and backgrounds. I thoroughly enjoy problem solving, thinking out of the box and adapt well to change and the demands of the workforce. Currently, I am studying Front-End Development to improve my expertise with knowledge and experience with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. In addition, I actively learn and experiment with new coding techniques. I hold an Associate Degree in Applied Business, as well as Udacity certification for a Front-End Nanodegree.
